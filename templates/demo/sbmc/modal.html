Private Lessons

Lessons for Every Age and Level

South Bay Music Conservatory offers private lessons in piano, violin, viola, and cello for students of all ages — from young beginners to adults returning after a long break. Our experienced faculty tailor each lesson to your student's individual goals, pace, and learning style.

Lesson lengths available: 15, 30, 45, and 60 minutes. Trial lessons available for new students.
