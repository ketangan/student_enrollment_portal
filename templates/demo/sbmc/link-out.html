Private Lessons

Lessons for Every Age and Level

South Bay Music Conservatory offers private lessons in piano, violin, viola, and cello — year-round. Our experienced faculty work with students of all ages and backgrounds, from young beginners to adult learners and advanced students preparing for college auditions.

New students are welcome to start with a 30-minute trial lesson to meet a teacher and experience the program.

Submit your enrollment inquiry online. Our team will follow up within 1–2 business days to discuss scheduling, lesson options, and next steps for your student.
