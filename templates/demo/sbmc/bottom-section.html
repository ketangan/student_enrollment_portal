Piano Lessons

Our private piano program welcomes students of all ages and backgrounds — from young beginners taking their very first lesson to accomplished players seeking advanced study. Each lesson is tailored to your student's goals, whether that means building strong fundamentals, exploring classical repertoire, learning popular music, or preparing for auditions and recitals.

All ages welcome — beginner through advanced.
Adult learners and transfer students encouraged to apply.
What to Expect
Weekly private lessons, structured home practice guidance, seasonal recitals, and ongoing teacher feedback.
