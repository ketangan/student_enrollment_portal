How Enrollment Works

  • 1

    Submit your inquiry

    Tell us about your student, instrument interest, and goals. It takes about 3 minutes.

    Submit Inquiry → Opens the full enrollment form on its own dedicated page
  • 2

    We'll reach out within 1–2 business days

    Our director will contact you to learn more and schedule a trial lesson.

  • 3

    Attend a 30-minute trial lesson ($30)

    Your student meets a teacher, tries the instrument, and we discuss fit and next steps.

  • 4

    Enroll and begin lessons

    Once enrolled, monthly billing begins. Recitals, events, and support are all included.
