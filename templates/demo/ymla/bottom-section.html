Admissions

Application Process

  • 1. Plan a Visit

    Come take a tour of our preschool to see what we have to offer. Tours are conducted on a regular basis for prospective parents to get a general overview of our teaching style and facilities.

  • 2. Get on our Wait List

    If you decide our preschool is right for you, fill out an application to be placed on our waitlist. There is a $100 application fee to get on the wait list. You must tour our school first. ***This application DOES NOT guarantee a spot at our preschool.

  • 3. Enrollment

    Our new school year begins in July. Enrollment for the school year begins in January of that year. We contact families on our waitlist between January–April notifying you of our available spots. Please look out for an email from us.

  • 4. Pre-Attendance Visits

    Prior to your child's first day of school, we encourage you to come for 1–4 "play dates" on our playground, allowing your child to get acquainted with their new school, friends, and teachers.

  • 5. First Week of School

    We gradually introduce new students into the program so they feel safe and comfortable. We ask parents to dedicate one week of their time to help your child transition into preschool.
