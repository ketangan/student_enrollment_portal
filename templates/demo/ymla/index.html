Enrollment Form — Integration Options

Below are four ways to embed or link your enrollment form into the Young Minds Learning Academy website. Click any option to see a live preview.

Option 1

Dedicated Enrollment Page

The form lives on its own page — youngmindsla.com/apply or similar. Clean, distraction-free, and the simplest to implement. Parents land directly on the form with no other page content to scroll past.

View Demo →
Option 2 — Recommended

Modal / Lightbox

The admissions page stays as-is. A button triggers a full-screen modal overlay with the form. No new page needed — works perfectly on top of your existing WordPress content. Best balance of minimal disruption and great UX.

View Demo →
Option 3

Inline Page Section

The form is embedded directly in a section at the bottom of your admissions page, below the existing 5-step process content. Parents don't leave the page — they scroll down and fill it out inline.

View Demo →
Option 4

Link Out (New Tab)

Buttons on your admissions page open the full enrollment form in a new browser tab. No iframe required — the simplest technical implementation and works perfectly on all devices including mobile.
