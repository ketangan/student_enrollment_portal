Enrollment Integration

How the enrollment form can fit into your website

Below are four ways your enrollment form can be added to your existing website. Every option uses the same form and the same admissions workflow — the only difference is how families reach it.

Most seamless for families: fully embedded on the admissions page.
Fastest to set up: a dedicated page or a link out.

What stays the same

Your branding, your application questions, your admissions review workflow, and your reporting all stay exactly as they are. These options only change how the form is presented to families on your website.

What this preview shows

Each option below places the same enrollment form into your site a different way, so you can compare what feels most natural for families and easiest for your team to maintain.

Option 1

Dedicated application page

Clean and distraction-free

Families click "Apply" and go straight to a full application page. This keeps the experience simple, focused, and easy to manage.

  • Best when you want the form on its own page
  • Very simple to implement and maintain
  • Strong choice for mobile visitors and direct links
View demo → Best for: simplicity
Option 2

Pop-up form on your existing page

Minimal change to your current site

Your current admissions page stays mostly as-is. Families click a button and the application opens in a pop-up over the page.

  • Keeps your current page content intact
  • Feels lighter than sending families to a new page
  • Good when you want only minimal website edits
View demo → Best for: minimal disruption
Recommended

Fully embedded into the admissions page

Most seamless experience for families

The application appears directly within your admissions page, below your existing process content. Families stay on one page and the form feels like a natural part of the site.

  • Feels the most native to your website
  • Best overall experience for families
  • Strongest option for reducing drop-off
View demo → Best for: family experience
Option 4

Open the form in a new tab

Fastest technical setup

Your admissions page includes a button that opens the full application in a separate browser tab. This is the fastest, lowest-effort option to launch.

  • Very easy to launch quickly
  • No embedded layout work needed
  • Works well across desktop and mobile
View demo → Best for: fastest rollout
Bonus Option

Interest / Inquiry Form

Capture leads before they're ready to apply

Families who aren't ready to fill out a full application can still express interest. The lightweight inquiry form captures their name, email, and program interest — and their record appears immediately in your Leads admin for follow-up.

  • Much shorter than the enrollment form (name, email, message)
  • Embeddable on a Contact Us or Programs page
  • Works alongside any of the four enrollment options above
View lead form → Embed preview → Best for: top-of-funnel capture

Our recommendation for your school

For most schools, the fully embedded option is the strongest fit. It preserves the admissions content you already have, keeps families on a single page, and feels the most natural within your existing site design. If you'd prefer the simplest possible launch, the dedicated application page is an excellent second choice — and you can always move to the embedded version later.
