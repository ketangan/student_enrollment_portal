How to Apply

Our Application Process

  • 1

    Explore Our Programs

    Review our program offerings to find the right fit for your student. Our admissions team is happy to answer questions about curriculum, schedule, and availability.

  • 2

    Submit Your Application

    Complete the enrollment form online. The application takes about 10 minutes and covers your student's information, program interest, and scheduling preferences.

    Opens in a pop-up window — stay on this page
  • 3

    Application Review

    Our admissions team reviews all submissions carefully. You'll hear from us within 2–3 business days with next steps or any follow-up questions.

  • 4

    Enrollment Confirmation

    Once your application is approved, you'll receive a welcome packet with everything your family needs to prepare for your student's first day.
