How It Works

  • 1

    Tell us about your student

    Fill out our short enrollment interest form below. It takes about 5 minutes and helps us understand your student's grade level, goals, and schedule.

    Request Enrollment → Form is embedded below on this page
  • 2

    We review and reach out

    Our team reviews every request within 1–2 business days and follows up personally with program options and scheduling availability.

  • 3

    Schedule a consultation

    We'll set up a quick call or visit to match your student with the right educator and program format.

  • 4

    Get matched with your program

    Tutoring, homeschool support, teen workshops, or life coaching — we'll confirm the best fit and set up your student's first session.

  • 5

    Start learning

    Your student begins with a dedicated DUC educator who understands their specific needs and goals.
