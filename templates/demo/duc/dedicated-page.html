Enrollment & Programs

How It Works

  • 1

    Tell us about your student

    Fill out our short enrollment interest form. It takes about 5 minutes and helps us understand your student's grade level, goals, and schedule preferences.

    Request Enrollment → Opens the full enrollment form on its own dedicated page
  • 2

    We review and reach out

    Our team reviews every request within 1–2 business days and follows up personally with next steps, program options, and scheduling availability.

  • 3

    Schedule a consultation

    We'll set up a quick call or in-person visit to better understand your student's needs and match them with the right educator and program format.

  • 4

    Get matched with your program

    Whether it's 1-on-1 tutoring, homeschool support, a teen workshop, or life coaching — we confirm the best fit and set up your student's first session.

  • 5

    Start learning

    Your student begins their program with a dedicated DUC educator who understands their specific learning needs and goals.
