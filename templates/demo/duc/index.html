How the enrollment form can fit into your website

Below are four ways the DUC Learning Center enrollment interest form can be added to your existing website. Each option uses the same form and the same admissions workflow — the only difference is how families access it.

Most seamless parent experience: fully embedded on your Programs or Enrollment page.
Fastest setup: link out or dedicated page.

What stays the same

Your branding, enrollment questions, student records, and follow-up workflow all stay the same. These options only change how the form is presented to families on your website.

What this demo is showing

Each preview below shows the same DUC enrollment form placed into the site in a different way, so you can compare what feels most natural for families and easiest for your team to manage.

Option 1

Dedicated enrollment page

Clean and distraction-free

Families click "Enroll" and go straight to a full enrollment page. Simple, focused, and easy to share — works well as a direct link from email or social media.

  • Best when you want the form on its own page
  • Very simple to implement and maintain
  • Strong choice for mobile and direct links
View demo → Best for: simplicity
Option 2

Pop-up form on your existing page

Minimal change to the current site

Your current Programs or Enrollment page stays mostly as-is. Families click a button and the enrollment form opens in a modal overlay.

  • Keeps your current page content intact
  • Feels lighter than sending families to a new page
  • Good if you want minimal website edits
View demo → Best for: minimal disruption
Recommended

Fully embedded into the enrollment page

Most seamless experience for families

The enrollment form appears directly within your Enrollment page, below your program descriptions. Families stay on one page and the form feels like a natural extension of your site.

  • Feels the most native to the DUC website
  • Best overall experience for parents and students
  • Strongest option for reducing friction and drop-off
View demo → Best for: parent experience
Option 4

Open the form in a new tab

Fastest technical implementation

Your enrollment page includes a button that opens the full form in a separate browser tab. This is the fastest and lowest-effort option to get started.

  • Very easy to launch quickly
  • No embedded layout work needed
  • Works well across desktop and mobile
View demo → Best for: fastest rollout
Bonus Option

Interest / Inquiry Form

Capture leads before families are ready to enroll

A short inquiry form — name, email, program, message — for families who are researching but not yet ready to submit a full enrollment request. Responses appear instantly in the DUC admin Leads page for follow-up.

  • Much lighter than the enrollment form
  • Embeddable on the Contact or Programs page
  • Feeds directly into the lead follow-up workflow
View inquiry form → Embed preview → Best for: top-of-funnel

Recommendation for DUC Learning Center

Based on the current site structure, the fully embedded option is the strongest fit. It preserves your existing program descriptions, keeps families on one page, and gives the enrollment form a professional, permanent home without requiring a separate application site.
