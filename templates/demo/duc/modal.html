Enrollment & Programs

How It Works

  • 1

    Tell us about your student

    Fill out our short enrollment interest form. It takes about 5 minutes and helps us understand your student's grade level, goals, and schedule.

  • 2

    Submit your enrollment request

    Click the button below to open the enrollment form. It stays right on this page as a pop-up — no need to navigate away.

    Opens the form in a pop-up on this page
  • 3

    We review and reach out

    Our team reviews every request within 1–2 business days and follows up with personalized next steps and program options.

  • 4

    Schedule a consultation & get matched

    We'll confirm the right program and set up your student's first session — whether that's 1-on-1 tutoring, homeschool support, a teen workshop, or life coaching.

  • 5

    Start learning

    Your student begins with a dedicated DUC educator who understands their specific needs and goals.

Ready to get started?

Submit your enrollment interest form and we'll follow up within 1–2 business days.
