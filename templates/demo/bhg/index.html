How the registration form can fit into your website

Below are four ways the Beverly Hills Gymnastics Center class registration form can be added to your existing website. Each option uses the same form and the same admissions workflow — the only difference is how families access it.

Most seamless parent experience: fully embedded on your Classes or Registration page.
Fastest setup: link out or dedicated page.

What stays the same

Your branding, registration questions, gymnast records, and follow-up workflow all stay the same. These options only change how the form is presented to families on your website.

What this demo is showing

Each preview below shows the same BHGC registration form placed into the site in a different way, so you can compare what feels most natural for families and easiest for your team to manage.

Option 1

Dedicated registration page

Clean and distraction-free

Families click "Register" and go straight to a full registration page. Simple, focused, and easy to share directly from social media or email campaigns.

  • Best when you want the form on its own page
  • Simple to implement and maintain
  • Strong choice for mobile and direct links
View demo → Best for: simplicity
Option 2

Pop-up form on your existing page

Minimal change to the current site

Your current Classes or Registration page stays mostly as-is. Families click "Register Now" and the form opens in a modal overlay.

  • Keeps your current page content intact
  • Feels lighter than sending families to a new page
  • Good if you want minimal website edits
View demo → Best for: minimal disruption
Recommended

Fully embedded into the registration page

Most seamless experience for families

The registration form appears directly within your Classes or Registration page, below your program descriptions. Families stay on one page and the form feels like a natural extension of your site.

  • Feels the most native to the BHGC website
  • Best overall experience for parents
  • Strongest option for reducing friction and drop-off
View demo → Best for: parent experience
Option 4

Open the form in a new tab

Fastest technical implementation

Your registration page includes a button that opens the full form in a separate browser tab. The fastest and lowest-effort option to get started.

  • Very easy to launch quickly
  • No embedded layout work needed
  • Works well across desktop and mobile
View demo → Best for: fastest rollout
Bonus Option

Interest / Inquiry Form

Capture families who aren't ready to register yet

A lightweight inquiry form — name, email, program interest, message — for families exploring options before they're ready to submit a full registration. Responses appear instantly in your admin Leads pipeline for follow-up.

  • Much shorter than the full registration form
  • Embeddable on a Contact or Classes page
  • Feeds directly into the lead follow-up workflow
View inquiry form → Embed preview → Best for: top-of-funnel

Recommendation for Beverly Hills Gymnastics Center

Based on the current site structure, the fully embedded option is the strongest fit. It preserves your existing program descriptions and class schedule content, keeps families on one page, and gives the registration form a permanent, professional home without requiring a separate application site.
