Classes & Programs

Programs for ages 18 months to adult. Open enrollment and placement-required classes available year-round.

Our Programs

Ages 3–4

Mini-Tots

An introductory gymnastics program for young children. No parent required in the gym. Focuses on body awareness, coordination, and fun.

Open Enrollment
Ages 5–7

Girls Beginning Gymnastics

Pre-Level 1 instruction following USAG guidelines. Cartwheels, rolls, balances, and introductory bar and beam work.

Placement Evaluation
Ages 7–10

Girls Intermediate Gymnastics

For gymnasts with prior experience. Builds on foundational skills with increased time on apparatus. Min. 90 min/week.

Placement Evaluation
Ages 10+

Girls Advanced Gymnastics

Final recreational level before joining the competitive team. Advanced skill development on all four apparatus.

Placement Evaluation
Ages 5–8

Boys Beginning Gymnastics

Introduction to men's gymnastics — floor, rings, bars, and pommel horse foundations in a supportive group setting.

Placement Evaluation
All Ages

Tumbling For All Ages

Standalone tumbling class for cheerleaders and athletes of all backgrounds. Cartwheels, handstands, round-offs, and back walkovers.

Open Enrollment
Adults

Adult Gymnastics

Co-ed classes for adults at all skill levels. A welcoming environment for those returning to gymnastics or trying it for the first time.

Open Enrollment
Ages 3–7

Competition Pre-Team — Bronze

For gymnasts showing competitive potential. 4 hours/week. USAG-track training with structured skill progression toward meets.

Coach Recommendation Required
All Ages

Private Lessons

One-on-one coaching in the facility. Ideal for skill development, injury recovery, or personalized attention alongside a group program.
