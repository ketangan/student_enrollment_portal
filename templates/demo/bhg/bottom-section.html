Girls Beginning Gymnastics

Ages 5–7 Placement Evaluation Required

Our Girls Beginning program is designed for gymnasts ages 5–7 who are new to the sport or have minimal experience. Following USAG Pre-Level 1 guidelines, students develop a strong foundation in body mechanics, spatial awareness, and introductory skills across all four apparatus.

Skills Covered
  • Forward and backward rolls
  • Cartwheels and round-offs
  • Introductory beam balances and walks
  • Bar supports and swings
  • Handstand progressions
Class Format
Small group classes (6–8 gymnasts per coach) held at our Olympic-standard Beverly Hills facility. Students rotate through floor, vault, beam, and bars each session. Classes are 60–75 minutes depending on the schedule.
Schedule & Pricing
Classes held Monday through Saturday. Multiple time slots available. Pricing starts at $266/month (2-month minimum commitment). Exact schedule confirmed after placement evaluation.
What to Bring
Leotard required (no shorts or skirts over). Hair must be secured away from face. No jewelry. Bare feet in the gym — no socks. Water bottle recommended.
About the Placement Evaluation A brief evaluation with one of our coaches is required before joining this class. It typically takes 15–20 minutes and helps us place your child at the right level. After you submit the registration form below, our team will schedule the evaluation and confirm availability.
