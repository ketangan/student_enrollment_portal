How Registration Works

  • 1

    Submit your registration

    Complete our class registration form. It takes about 5 minutes and helps us understand your gymnast's age, experience level, and scheduling preferences.

    Register Now → Opens the full registration form on its own dedicated page
  • 2

    We follow up within 1–2 business days

    Our team reviews every registration and reaches out personally to confirm scheduling, program placement, and pricing details.

  • 3

    Placement evaluation (for skill-based programs)

    For Girls Intermediate, Advanced, Competition Team, and similar programs, a brief coach evaluation ensures your gymnast is placed in the right level from day one.

  • 4

    Confirm your schedule and start training

    Once placed, you'll receive a class schedule, pricing details, and the required registration waiver — then your gymnast is ready to start.

Ready to get started?

Submit your registration and our team will follow up within 1–2 business days.
