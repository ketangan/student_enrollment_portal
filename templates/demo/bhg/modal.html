Classes & Programs

Programs for Every Level

Beverly Hills Gymnastics Center offers programs for ages 18 months to adult. Open-enrollment classes are available immediately. Placement-required programs include a brief coach evaluation to ensure your gymnast is placed at the right level.

Classes are held Monday through Sunday. Pricing starts at $266/month (2-month minimum).
